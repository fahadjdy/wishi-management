WISHI

Digital Committee & Savings-Group Management

What is WISHI?

WISHI is an easy-to-use platform for running group savings committees — also commonly known as a "BC", a "chit fund", or a "savings circle".

A committee is a group of trusted people who each put in a fixed amount of money on a regular schedule (for example, every month). In each round, all the collected money is given to one member. This continues, round after round, until every member has received the pool once.

In one line: WISHI takes the whole committee — the money, the members, the turns, and the records — and keeps it organised online, so everyone can trust it.

The problem it solves

Running a committee by hand is stressful and easy to get wrong:

  • Hard to keep track of who has paid and who is still pending.
  • Arguments over whose turn it is to receive the money.
  • No clear, lasting record of past payments and payouts.
  • Trust issues when cash is handled informally.
  • Endless manual reminders, totals, and calculations.

WISHI removes all of this by moving the entire committee online — with clear records, fair selection, and a complete history that everyone can see.

How it works

Create a committeeSet the number of members, the contribution amount, and how often it runs.
Add membersThe organiser adds or invites people to join the group.
Collect contributionsEach round, members pay their fixed amount — every payment is recorded.
Pick the winnerOne member is chosen to receive the pool — by a fair draw or by bidding.
Pay outThe chosen member receives the collected money, recorded in the system.
RepeatThe rounds continue until every member has had their turn.

Key features

Easy committee setup

Create a committee in minutes — choose the size, the amount, the schedule, and the rules that suit your group.

Member management

Add, invite, approve, or remove members. Only the organiser can add people, so the group stays trusted.

Fair random draw

Pick each round's winner with a transparent lottery that is recorded — so no one can dispute the result.

Bidding option

Members can bid for an earlier turn, and the amount saved is shared fairly with the whole group.

Payment tracking

See at a glance who has paid and who is still pending in every round — no more guesswork.

Personal payment history

Every member can view their own complete payment timeline across all rounds.

Payouts & records

Record money given to winners, including partial payouts and organiser top-ups when needed.

Member trust score

Each member builds a credit/trust score based on paying on time — so the organiser knows who is reliable.

Dashboards

A clear overview for members, plus a separate control panel for the organiser to manage everything.

Notifications

Members are alerted about important actions, so nobody misses a payment or an update.

Full activity history

Every action is logged with the date and the person — keeping the whole committee transparent and accountable.

Built-in security

Accounts lock after repeated wrong passwords, and sensitive actions are protected from misuse.
